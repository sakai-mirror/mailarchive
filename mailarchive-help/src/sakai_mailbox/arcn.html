Deleting an Email Archive message

Note: To complete the procedures in this document, you must be assigned a role having the necessary permissions. To determine your role, follow the directions in Participant roles. For a basic understanding of roles and permissions, see Permissions and roles: Overview.

To delete an Email Archive message, follow these steps:

  1. In the menubar of the relevant course site or project site, click Email Archive.

  2. Click the linked subject of the message you want to delete to open it, and then click Delete.

  3. Click Remove to confirm the deletion.
